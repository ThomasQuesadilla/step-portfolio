Thomas Quintanilla

Weclome! My name is Thomas Quintanilla. I'm a rising junior at Duke Univeristy and a current STEP intern at Google. I'm a results driven programmer who strives to build applications that will suit the needs of everyone. My work revolves mostly around object oriented programming and applications for commodity use. My main goal though is to eventually work on VR/AR and help develop the field so that it may be more practical for the average consumer to use.

What makes me unique is the good vibes I can bring to any group. Whether its through my stupid shenanigans or uplifting conversations, I can usually turn around the foul moods of almost any group I'm a part of. Honestly, I'm not entirely sure where it originally came from, but I feel like my past experiences helped me develop a sense of empathy with those I'm close with, so I naturally never want to see anyone down or upset.
